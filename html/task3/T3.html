Task 3

Find the missing nodes between two graphs

In this task, you will be given two graphs side by side. The graphs are identical, except that the graph on the right will always be missing FOUR nodes, as well as missing links associated with those nodes. In this task, you must identify which nodes are missing from the right graph, and then select those missing nodes in the left graph.


You can manipulate the LEFT graph inside its border, and the right graph will move in the same way. If you accidentally select a node, select it again to deselect that node. When you are ready, click CONTINUE.
