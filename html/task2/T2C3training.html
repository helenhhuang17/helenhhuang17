Condition: Trackball Mouse, 3D Graph

Condition : Trackball Mouse, 3D Graph

To operate the trackball mouse:
  • To rotate the graph, move your cursor to the gray section of the screen and left click. Your pointer will disappear and you can rotate the object using the trackball.
  • To get the pointer back, press ESC on the keyboard.
  • To zoom in and out, use the grey scroll ring around the blue ball on the mouse.
  • To pan around the graph, left click while rolling the blue ball with your fingers.
When you are ready, click PRACTICE to begin the practice trials.
